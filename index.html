Melendi

The man who remade Rumba

Melendi performancing in Brusellas in tour "Volvamos a empezar".

Here's a timeline of Melendi's life:

  • 1979 - born in Oviedo Asturias.
  • 2001 - Joined a group called "El bosque de Sherwood".
  • 2003 - Released his first solo disc.
  • 2004 - His song called "Con la luna llena" was chosen as official theme of "Vuelta a España".
  • 2005 - Released his second solo disc "Que el cielo espere sentao".
  • 2006 - He played "Mr. Big" in PSP game "Gangs of London".
  • 2007 - Received "Best Tour" award from "Premios de la Música".

"Y es que tengo un problema, voy de bar en peor, si el problema es sin remedio yo aqui tengo gramo y medio, compartamos el dolor".
